About Me.

18 year old from Los Angeles, California. Growing up I was always involved in the music scene. Going to different concerts every weekend, my love for music has only grown stronger as I've gotten older.
I started playing music around the age of ten. My dad always had guitars laying around the house and one day I decided to pick one up and start playing. From there, I began writing my own music: And that's where it all began...
